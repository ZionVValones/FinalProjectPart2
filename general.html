General Actions

Reduce Energy Use

You can use appliances that save energy and consume less electricity.

Use Sustainable Transportation: Walking or cycling

You can use appliances that save energy and consume less electricity.

Minimize Waste and Conserve Water

You may reduce your waste by performing the 5rs (reduce, reuse, recycle, recover and repair) and by using fewer single-use plastics. In addition, you can save water by fixing leaks and turning off the faucet when not in use.

Encourage the Use of Renewable Energy

Choose energy sources like wind and solar power.

Advocate and educate

Raise awareness about climate change.

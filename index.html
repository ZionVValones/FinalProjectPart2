About this Website

The temperature of our world is increasing, sea levels are rising and glaciers are melting. This is the evidence of climate change we are now experiencing. Everyone must take part in climate action because this will affect the future's situation. This website is based on SDG 13 and shows actions that today's generation are encouraged to do on the specific measures that an adult, student, and child can do for a sustainable environment and climate.

General Climate Action

Everyone has the opportunity to participate in climate action, regardless of age or background. A major change can be achieved by reducing garbage, water consumption, energy use, and by using sustainable transportation. Furthermore, one of the most important ways to address the current global problem is to support policies that have an impact on climate change. Together, the people of the world may fight climate change and save the environment.
